Recent Projects

I love developing products and solutions that create value to general masses. I believe computers will play a huge role in solving problems that concern us at a global level.

TextExchange.io

A Textbook exchange service for UW students (Over 200 registered users). The website indexes books by classes taught at UW for fast and efficient retrieval of posts making it easy to compare prices for different books and contact sellers. Also developed a notification framework that emails a user when someone posts a book they were looking for.

unison.news

Crowd-sourced news reader for visually challenged with a Chrome Web App that enables readers to record their voice and a website with a voice user interface for listeners. Served as a Product Manager on the team and also wrote the backend along with technical specs for the website and the Chrome app.

Hangman++

A Web implementation of the UW CSE 143's "Evil Hangman".
The software maintains a list of every word in the English language and continuously pares down the word list to try to dodge the player's guesses as much as possible.
